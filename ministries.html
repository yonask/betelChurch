Ministries

Use your gifts to serve others.

“Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others.” — 1 Peter 4:10

Grow and serve

Ministry teams and leadership

Our ministries exist to glorify God, build believers, serve our community, and advance the Gospel through teamwork, love, and faithful service.

01

Leadership Board

Provides prayerful spiritual direction, biblical guidance, vision, oversight, unity, and support for pastors and ministry leaders.

Acts 20:28
02

Deacons Ministry

Serves practical needs of the church, cares for members and families, supports programs, and serves with integrity and love.

1 Timothy 3:13
03

Sunday School Ministry

Teaches children God’s Word, encourages prayer and Christian character, and partners with parents in spiritual growth.

Proverbs 22:6
04

Youth Ministry

Helps young people know Christ, grow spiritually, build community, and discover their God-given gifts.

1 Timothy 4:12
05

Bible Study Groups

Helps believers study Scripture, pray for one another, build accountability, and apply God’s Word to daily life.

Acts 2:42
06

Worship Ministry

Leads the congregation in worship through music and praise, serving with prayer, excellence, and humility.

Psalm 100:2
07

Sound System Team

Supports worship and preaching through reliable sound, equipment care, recording, and faithful behind-the-scenes service.

Colossians 3:23
08

Women’s Ministry

Encourages women through prayer, fellowship, biblical encouragement, family support, spiritual growth, and community service.

Proverbs 31:26
09

Men’s Ministry

Encourages men to grow as disciples, husbands, fathers, mentors, and servants of Christ.

1 Corinthians 16:13
10

Prayer Ministry

Strengthens the church through intercession, prayer gatherings, care, and faith-filled support for people in need.

1 Thessalonians 5:17

Why ministry matters

Every believer has a role in the life of the church. We want each person to grow in faith, discover their calling, and serve with joy.

  • Serve with your God-given gifts
  • Build community and spiritual maturity
  • Help strengthen the church and our outreach
