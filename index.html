About Our Church

We are a church that believes in Jesus, loves God, and serves people.

Bethel Ethiopian Evangelical Church is a Christ-centered community committed to worship, biblical teaching, prayer, discipleship, generosity, and outreach.

Whether you are new to faith, returning to church, or looking for a spiritual home, we would love to welcome you.
