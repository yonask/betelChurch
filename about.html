About Us

One Church. One Family. One Mission.

Our Story

We exist to help people know Jesus and make Him known.

Grace Church began with a small group of believers who wanted to create a welcoming spiritual home for worship, discipleship, prayer, and service.

Our mission is simple: love God, love people, and serve the world with the hope of the Gospel.
